Welcome to Your Seedr Guide

This site helps you use Seedr for seamless cloud downloading, with tips on uploading files, creating torrents, and working with cloud links.

What is Seedr?

Seedr is a powerful cloud-based downloader that lets you fetch torrents and direct download links to your private storage, then stream or download them instantly. Visit Seedr

Getting Started

  • Upload magnet or torrent links to Seedr
  • Use direct HTTP/HTTPS links for public files
  • Stream or download completed files to your device
